ABOUT US?

At The Barking Lot, we are passionate about providing every dog with a loving home. Founded with the mission to rehabilitate and rehome dogs in need, we have been a safe haven for countless furry friends. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that every dog receives the care, attention, and affection they deserve.

Our mission is to strive to create a world where every dog has a second chance at happiness. Through rescue efforts, medical care, and adoption services, we work tirelessly to match each dog with the perfect family. Our mission is not just about finding homes but creating lifelong bonds between dogs and their adopters.

WHO ARE WE?

We are a team of dog lovers, advocates, and volunteers dedicated to making a difference in the lives of our furry friends. From shelters and rescues to individuals looking for a safe place to rehome their pets, we work with a network of trusted partners to ensure every dog gets the care and attention they deserve.

WHAT DO WE DO?

RESCUE
REHABILITATION
REHOME
EDUCATE
We save dogs from neglect, abandonment, and high-risk situations.
Our team provides medical care, training, and socialization to help dogs thrive.
We connect our dogs with loving families, ensuring a perfect match for both parties.
We promote responsible pet ownership, including spaying/neutering, proper care, and the importance of adoption.

WHY CHOOSE US?

When you adopt here from The Barking Lot, you’re not just giving a dog a home—you’re saving a life. We ensure every dog is healthy, vaccinated, and ready to thrive in their new environment. Our team is here to support you every step of the way, from the moment you find your match to the day you bring your new friend home.

Join us in our mission to make the world a better place for dogs, one adoption at a time. Together, we can create a brighter future for our four-legged companions.
